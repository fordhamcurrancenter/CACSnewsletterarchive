October/November 2023 Monthly Update

Catholic Studies News

We are delighted to extend our warmest congratulations to our 35 newly accepted Class of 2026 American Catholic Studies Concentrators!

From October 13th to October 21st, 7 of our concentrators as well as our director, Dr. Michael Lee, travelled to the beautiful city of Rome to attend the Synod on Synodality. Fordham’s delegation was made up of 11 students and 4 faculty members in total.

On November 3rd, we had the joy to see the powerful production of Brian Friel's "Translations" at the historic Irish Repertory Theatre, along with 30 CACS concentrators. It was an added delight to meet the actors afterwards and enjoy the best Irish Pub in Chelsea!

CACS Events

The Enduring Legacy of the UCA Martyrs

The Curran Center Commemorated the 34th anniversary of the UCA Martyrs in a 2 Part Event

Screening of Llegaron de Noche

On November 1st, we hosted a screening of Llegaron de Noche, which was a powerful dramatic portrayal of Lucia Cerna and her brave testimony as a witness to the murder of the UCA martyrs.

The Enduring Legacy of the UCA Martyrs

On November 2nd, we had the honor of hosting Fr. José María Tojeira, SJ  who was the Jesuit Provincial at the time of the UCA martyrs. His lecture shared stories about these remarkable figures, and reflected on how their legacy continues to challenge us today.

Paradoxical Reformations in Latin America: Catholics, Evangelicals, and the Crisis of Democracy

On November 13th, Raúl E. Zegarra, Ph.D., winner of the 2023 New Scholar Essay Prize for Catholic Studies in the Americas presented his talk on the current political crisis in Latin America.

Curran Center Faculty Seminar: Laudate Deum

On November 13th, we hosted our final virtual session of our faculty seminar, where we discussed Laudate Deum, the brief exhortation from Pope Francis published as a follow-up to Laudato Si in October 2023. Facilitated by Dr. Angela Alaimo O’Donnell and presented by facultly Jason Morris (Biology Dept.), Sean Collins (Economics Dept.), and Christiana Zenner (Theology Dept.) 

[Co-Sponsored Event] The Art of Encounter: Catholic Writers from the Margins


From October 20th to October 21st, we co-sponsored the Catholic Writers fom the Margins conference featuring Angela Alaimo O'Donnell, Pádraig Ó Tuama, Alice McDermott, and many more talented writers hosted at Boston College!

[Co-Sponsored Event] Poets of Presence: Faith, Form, and Foraging Community


From October 27th to 28th, we co-sponsored the Poets of Presence conference hosted at The Hank Center at Loyola University Chicago!
